BIOGRAPHY
May 06 2024

Yuteng Yan, presently a Ph.D. candidate in Demography at Renmin University of China under the mentorship of Professor Ting Li, focuses on Health Research, Family Research, and Social Research Methods. He is dedicated to leveraging big data and micro simulation models to enhance public comprehension of the human condition and promote social equity. His curriculum vitae is accessible for download in both English and Chinese versions.
