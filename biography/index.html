BIOGRAPHY
Mar 29 2023

Yuteng Yan is currently a Ph.D. student in Demography, supervised by Prof. Ting Li at Renmin University of China. His main research interests include Health Research, Family Research, and Social Research Methods. Now, he is passionate about using big data and micro agent-based models to help the common to better understand the human condition and advance social equity. His resume is available for download (Engish CV or Chinese CV).
